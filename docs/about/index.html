About us: Mission and Offerings

What We Offer

LoopMaster provides a simple yet powerful platform that includes:

Customizable Practice Sessions:

Set the number of images and timer for your practice session to suit your needs.

Feedback Options:

Opt for immediate feedback after each drawing to quickly correct mistakes or receive a comprehensive review at the end of the session to see your overall progress.

New Shapes for Every Practice:

Every time you practice, a new image of shapes is generated just for you from our exclusive 3D assets, ensuring a unique experience that allows you to explore and refine your artistic skills.

Our Mission

At LoopMaster, our mission is to help artists develop a deeper understanding of form and structure through consistent practice. We believe that consistency is the key to success, and our platform is designed to facilitate regular, focused practice sessions that enhance your ability to visualize and construct 3D shapes.
effectively.

Our Inspiration

As artists, we saw a gap in resources for practicing 3D form construction. While many platforms focus on gesture drawing and basic shapes, few cater to cross-contour line drawing, essential for visualizing three-dimensional structures. Our daily practice revealed the need for a dedicated platform that combines structure and flexibility, enabling artists to enhance their skills effectively.

Thank you for choosing LoopMaster.

Let's draw the cross-contour line or loops and unlock the full potential of your illustrations together!
